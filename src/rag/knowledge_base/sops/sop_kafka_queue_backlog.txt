SOP: Kafka Queue Backlog and DLQ Growth
Category: Queue backlog | Severity: P2 | Owner: Data Platform Team

Symptoms:
- Consumer lag rising
- DLQ growth detected
- Retry topic depth increasing
- Poison message key observed
- Rebalance loop

Diagnostics:
- Check consumer lag by topic and partition
- Inspect DLQ samples for poison-message pattern
- Verify consumer pod restarts and rebalance frequency
- Confirm producer rate versus consumer throughput

Remediation:
- Scale consumers only when no poison message is active
- Pause producer or quarantine poison messages if retry storm is active
- Scale consumers to zero before skipping a poison offset
- Replay DLQ only after parser or schema fix is deployed

Validation:
- Consumer lag decreases
- DLQ growth stops
- Rebalance frequency returns to normal
- Consumer processing rate exceeds producer rate

Safety:
- Do not replay poison messages before fixing the parser
- Require data-platform approval before skipping offsets
