WHAT audit-service DOES
------------------------
audit-service records state transitions for financial positions in the alpha-capital platform.
Key state transitions it tracks: PENDING → ACTIVE → SETTLED → (normally end state)
Unexpected: SETTLED → PENDING_REVIEW (indicates a position being reopened for review)
audit_event=POSITION_RECONCILE: triggered when position state does not match expected ledger state

⚠️ create_ticket=false until root cause is confirmed. Raise investigation ticket only.
⚠️ audit-service signals alone are insufficient to act on — they indicate something upstream
   caused an unexpected state, but audit-service is the observer, not the cause.

Step 1 — Capture the full trace window:
  kubectl logs -l app=audit-service -n prod --since=30m | \
